Rules of the Tournament:

  • The discipline for the tournament is set to 5 vs 5.
  • After successfully filling out the form on the website, join the Discord server (for the entire team).
  • All the players are requested to register on the Fanclash Fantasy league.
  • The tournament will only be open to the first 64 teams that will get themselves registered through the website.
  • The format for the tournament is based on single elimination.
  • You can play in this tournament only if your registered and in-game names match, otherwise, you will be disqualified.
  • After registering, a player or team must agree to the rules and regulations of the tournament.
  • If any unforeseen circumstances arise, the tournament staff's decision is final.
  • Please show respect and decency to the commentators/presenters. Also, be respectful to one another. Any offensive or malicious messages or actions will result in disqualification
